Hello! Welcome to our LC 101 Project -- Pachinko! The famous ball drop game. The instructions are very simple, shoot the ball and try to land in one of the scoring buckets...

Except there is a catch to it... you have somehow get through the pegs!

The game Pachinko originated in Japan and is played mainly as a recreational arcade game or a gambling device. Pachinko is very similar to western styled pinball machines except Pachinko is a vertical pinball game. Players use little small balls to spring into the playing field, where it falls freely, hoping it lands in a scoring bucket. In our game, each bucket will count as a point, depending of degree of difficulty. There are different game modes:

  • 3-Ball
  • 5-ball
  • Pachinko!
